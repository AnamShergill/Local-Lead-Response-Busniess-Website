Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our AI lead response system for local businesses

How does the AI text-back system work for missed calls?

When a call is missed, our AI system immediately sends a personalized SMS to the caller within seconds. The automated text-back engages the lead, asks what they need, and can provide booking links or schedule callbacks - keeping them from calling your competitors.

Will this integrate with my existing CRM or scheduling system?

Yes! We integrate with major service business CRMs including ServiceTitan, Housecall Pro, Jobber, and most scheduling platforms via Zapier or native API connections. Your leads flow directly into your existing workflow.

How quickly can you install the AI lead response system?

Our team handles the entire installation process. Most local service businesses have their AI Text-Back or Voice Agent live and responding to leads within 3-5 business days. No technical knowledge required on your end.

What types of local businesses benefit most from AI lead response?

HVAC companies, plumbers, roofers, contractors, electricians, senior home care providers, med spas, dental clinics, and any local service business that receives customer calls. If you're losing leads to missed calls, our AI system will help.

Does the AI voice agent sound natural and professional?

Absolutely. Our AI voice agent uses advanced natural language processing to sound human and professional. It can handle basic questions, qualify leads, and book appointments while maintaining your brand's tone and professionalism.

Can I customize the AI responses for my specific business?

Yes! During setup, we train the AI with your business information, services, pricing, and common customer questions. The system learns your brand voice and can be updated anytime to match your evolving business needs.
